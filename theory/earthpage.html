Earth is the only known planet that supports life, thanks to its protective atmosphere and liquid water. Its magnetic field shields us from harmful solar radiation, and plate tectonics recycle nutrients essential to life. The perfect distance from the Sun allows for a stable climate and liquid oceans.

Origin of Life

Simple microscopic life appeared about 3.8 billion years ago. These were single-celled organisms like bacteria that lived in oceans. Over time, they evolved into more complex organisms, ultimately leading to plants, animals, and eventually humans. Photosynthetic bacteria began producing oxygen, slowly changing Earth's atmosphere.
